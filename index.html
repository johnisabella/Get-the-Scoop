GET THE SCOOP!

The scoop is a media-related word. Guess what it is before another journalist steals it!

Type any letter key on your keyboard to enter a guess. If your guess is correct, it will appear in the appropriate space (or spaces) below.

The competition for the scoop is fierce, and you only have six guesses to spare. After your sixth incorrect guess, the scoop will be lost and the game will be over.

Press enter at any time to start a new game.
